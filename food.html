🍕 Feed five people without losing your mind

Simple meals, portable snacks, and deal hunting.

The goal is not gourmet. It is predictable food, fewer drive-through emergencies, and choices that work before or after an outing.

One-trip grocery list

Easy meal rotation

  • Breakfast: waffle bar, cereal and fruit, eggs and toast, yogurt parfaits.
  • Lunch: sandwich boxes, quesadillas, snack plates, leftovers.
  • Dinner: tacos, spaghetti, sheet-pan nuggets and fries, pizza, breakfast-for-dinner.
  • Portable: turkey roll-ups, fruit, crackers, cheese, pretzels, applesauce pouches.
Make one snack bin: each child chooses two items before leaving. That prevents five separate pantry negotiations.
