Pasta night

Pasta, sauce, rotisserie chicken and carrots.

Fast-food deal shortcut

Deals vary by location and account. Before ordering for five people, check the restaurant app for family bundles, free-item offers and kids’ meal promos. Strong places to compare: McDonald’s, Wendy’s, Sonic, Chick-fil-A, Domino’s and Little Caesars.
