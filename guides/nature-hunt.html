Printable activity guide

Nature Bingo Hunt

Supplies

  • Printed list
  • Pencil
  • Water
  • Bug spray

Instructions

  1. Spot a bird, butterfly, ant, spider web, yellow flower, smooth leaf, rough bark, fallen branch, something shaped like a letter, something making noise, a cloud shape and an animal track.
  2. One item may count only once.
  3. Older kids photograph or sketch finds; younger kids point and name colors.
  4. Celebrate a completed row rather than requiring the full board.

Safety and setup notes

  • Look, photograph and sketch. Do not pick plants or touch wildlife.
