Printable activity guide

Indoor Scavenger Hunt

Supplies

  • Printed list or paper
  • Pencils
  • Optional timer

Instructions

  1. Find something red.
  2. Find something softer than a pillow.
  3. Find an object beginning with B.
  4. Find three circles.
  5. Find something that makes noise.
  6. Find something smaller than your thumb.
  7. Find an item everyone uses.
  8. Find two things that rhyme.
  9. Find something that smells good.
  10. Find an object older than one child.
  11. For younger kids, pair them with an older child and use pictures or colors instead of words.

Safety and setup notes

  • Do not hide or search near medicines, cleaning products or private belongings.
