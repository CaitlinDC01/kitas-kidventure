Farmers Market Scavenger Hunt

Supplies

  • This list
  • Pencil
  • Optional $5 child budget

Instructions

  1. Find one fruit, one vegetable, one baked item, one handmade object, one item grown in Texas, one food you have never tried, something purple and a vendor who can explain how something is made.
  2. Older children may politely ask one vendor a question.
  3. Each child votes on one item the family might buy.
  4. At the park, share the most interesting discovery.

Safety and setup notes

  • Ask before touching merchandise or taking photos.
