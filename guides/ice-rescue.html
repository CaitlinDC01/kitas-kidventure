Ice Rescue Mission

Supplies

  • Small plastic toys
  • Freezer-safe container
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Droppers or spoons
  • Warm water cups
  • Tray and towels

Instructions

  1. Freeze toys in a container of water the night before.
  2. Place the ice block on a tray.
  3. Give kids salt, droppers and small cups of warm water.
  4. Ask them to test which method frees toys fastest.
  5. Older children can record predictions and results.

Safety and setup notes

  • Use warm, not boiling, water. Avoid sharp tools.
