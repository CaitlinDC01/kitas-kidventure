Printable activity guide

Sidewalk Chalk City

Supplies

  • Sidewalk chalk
  • Toy cars or figures
  • Optional cardboard signs

Instructions

  1. Draw roads and intersections.
  2. Add a grocery store, hospital, park, school, restaurant and homes.
  3. Assign jobs: road builder, sign maker, architect and city planner.
  4. Use toy cars to act out deliveries, emergencies and trips.
  5. Finish with a “city tour” led by each child.

Safety and setup notes

  • Choose a safe driveway or sidewalk away from moving cars.
