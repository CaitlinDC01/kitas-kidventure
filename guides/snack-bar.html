Movie Snack Bar

Supplies

  • Popcorn
  • Pretzels or crackers
  • Fruit or applesauce
  • Small candy or cookies
  • Juice boxes or water
  • Paper cups or bowls
  • Play money or paper tickets

Instructions

  1. Give each child 10 pretend dollars.
  2. Price healthier items at $1, popcorn at $2 and candy/cookies at $3.
  3. Let one child be cashier while the others shop, then rotate.
  4. Everyone must choose a drink and at least one fruit before buying candy.
  5. Dim lights, collect a pretend movie ticket and start the feature.

Safety and setup notes

  • Use small portions so they can make several choices without overeating.
