🌦️ Quick decisions

Richmond 10-day weather

Heat advisory: choose museums, libraries, movies or water play before 11 a.m.

Storms: choose an at-home printable activity.

Everyone melting down: shorten the plan. A 60-minute win is better than forcing a four-hour outing.

🧒 Four-kid safety system

  1. Assign a buddy pair: one older child with one younger child.
  2. Take a quick group photo before entering a crowded place.
  3. Choose one obvious meetup spot immediately.
  4. Use a call-and-response phrase such as “Kidventure crew?” and “All together!”
  5. Count heads before leaving every area, entering the car and driving away.
Plans, pricing, weather, and streaming availability can change. Recheck linked sources before using them.
