Safety plan for four kids

  • Take a group photo at the start of each outing so Kita has a current clothing reference.
  • Give the older children a buddy assignment, without making them responsible for supervision.
  • Choose one visible meeting point immediately after arrival.
  • Teach the children to find a uniformed employee or a parent with children if separated.
  • Write Kita’s phone number on a paper wristband or card for the youngest children.

Fast decision rule

Green light: everyone slept, weather is manageable, supplies are packed. Do the planned outing.

Yellow light: someone is tired or the timing is slipping. Shorten the outing or choose a nearby option.

Red light: illness, dangerous heat, storms, or a meltdown already underway. Use the Home Fun page with no guilt.
